Classical dancer, mechanic, multifaceted aerospace personnel, now developer. If there is a challenge, I rise to it. To find out more about my archived or current projects, click the cards above to explore that area.

Bio:

My professional career started off as a classically trained ballet dancer where I was a featured performer in a traveling dance company. We performed on Broadway in NY, in Houston Texas, Chicago Michigan, and in various Rhode Island, Massachusets and Connecticut festivals. Simultaneously, I was attending trade school and started a career as an automotive technician attaining ASE and manufacturer certifications. As recently as 2020, I moved into the aerospace industry where i have had the chance to travel doing machine installation and repairs in areas like Rives-en-Seine, France and Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I also received training in customer service, laser and metal fabrication machine programming which is where I discovered how much I enjoy programming and development. Amongst other hobbies such as videography, #D Printing, and racecar building, I am always in search of opportunities to learn, grow and explore!
